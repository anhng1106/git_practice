Doraemon, a classic and beloved Japanese manga and anime series, was created by Fujiko F. Fujio. 
It follows the adventures of a young boy named Nobita Nobi, who struggles with grades and bullying. 
To aid Nobita, his future grandson sends back Doraemon, a blue robotic cat with a magical pocket. 
From this pocket, Doraemon can produce gadgets, tools, and medicines that help Nobita navigate the 
challenges of school and life. The series is celebrated for its mix of humor, life lessons, and the occasional 
touching moment, showcasing themes of friendship, perseverance, and the impact of technology on everyday 
life.
